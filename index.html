Hugues Van Assel

I am a PhD student in the department of Mathematics (UMPA) of Ecole Normale Supérieure de Lyon supervised by Aurélien Garivier and Titouan Vayer and in collaboration with Rémi Flamary and Nicolas Courty.

I work in the field of machine learning / data analysis. In my research, I develop unsupervised learning algorithms based on optimal transport and probabilistic modelling, focusing mainly on dimensionality reduction. I am particularly interested in biological applications (cellular biology, neuroscience …) of these methods.

Before starting my PhD, I was a student at Ecole polytechnique and MVA master. More informations can be found in my CV.
